01

Natalie Renaud

Beautiful Songs For Ugly Children (2021)

Drawing inspiration from a diverse range of genres and artists, Natalie weaves together elements of indie pop, folk, and alternative rock to create a sound that is both enchanting and evocative. Her ethereal vocals, accompanied by intricate guitar melodies and lush arrangements, transport listeners to a realm where emotions run deep and introspection reigns supreme.

Lorenzo James

The Brand New Renaissance (spring 2022)

With a voice that exudes warmth and depth, Lorenzo James effortlessly navigates the realms of R&B, funk, and gospel, infusing his music with a timeless elegance. His soul-stirring performances transport listeners to a bygone era, where the sounds of Motown and classic soul reigned supreme. Lorenzo's authentic and emotive delivery captivates audiences, evoking a range of emotions from joy to longing, and leaving them yearning for more.
